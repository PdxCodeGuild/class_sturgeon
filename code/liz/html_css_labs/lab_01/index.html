Alex Trebek

Alex Trebek (July 22, 1940 - November 8, 2020) was a Canadian-American game show host and television personality. He was best know for being the host of Jeopardy! (a syndicated general knowledge quiz show) for 37 seasons from 1984 until his death in 2020. He also hosted several other game shows including Classic Concentration and High Rollers.
