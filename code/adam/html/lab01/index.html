Naofumi Iwatani

20 y/o summoned to a parallel world along with 3x others to become global heroes and fight of moster waves. Equipped with a Legendary Shield, Naofumi earns the title of "Shield Hero" (盾の勇者, Tate no Yuusha) and goes on a journey with hatred towards society after being falsely accused of sexually assaulting a princess.

  • Born and raised: Tokyo, Japan
  • Currently Resides: Melromarc, Rock Valley
  • Status: Legendary Hero
  • Vocation: Merchant
  • Title: Archduke of Melromarc
