Berman Photography

Known for his images in fashion and environmentalism, Prius owner Adam Berman earned a full scholarship to film school after taking Silver at the Skills USA National Competition in 2016. His images have won numerous awards and have been featured in numerous art galleries in Salt Lake City, Utah.

Adam has worked in the film industry as a gaffer, grip, actor, director, and most notibly, as a cinematographer. His films have been shown in film festivals at the university, city, state, and underground levels. While a capable director, his passion is in lighting design and as director of photography. After finishing up at film school, Adam set out with a film crew and his long time photography mentor to build a portfolio by visiting 20 countries in 2020. He arrived in the Phillipines for New Years, and spent the year dodging covid/authorities throughout Asia.
